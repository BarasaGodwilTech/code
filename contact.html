Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Production projects usually take 2-4 weeks, while mixing and mastering can be completed in 1-2 weeks depending on complexity.

Do you offer remote services?

Yes! We work with artists worldwide through remote collaboration tools and file sharing platforms.

What file formats do you work with?

We work with all major DAW formats and can accept WAV, MP3, or stems for mixing and mastering.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for larger projects. Contact us to discuss options.
